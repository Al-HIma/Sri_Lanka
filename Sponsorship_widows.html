Sponsorship of Widows and Divorcees (كفالة الارامل)

Goal: To uplift the lives of 925 beneficiaries in need.

**Sponsor Now**

Project Summary: A Dignified Life for the Needy

The primary goal of this program is to improve the living standards of widows and divorced women. The Sponsorship of widows (كفالة الارامل) project aims to provide them with financial and social support to sustain their livelihoods after the loss of a spouse or following a divorce.

Types of Aid and Support Provided

1. Financial and Economic Support

  • Direct Financial Aid: Monthly financial assistance is deposited directly into their cards. (Monthly Sponsorship Value: $65 USD).
  • Needs Support: Fulfilling essential needs such as food (الطعام), clothing (الكساء), shelter, and hygiene.
  • Distribution: Distributing Zakat funds when necessary.

2. Professional and Employment Support

  • Vocational Training: Providing professional training to create a permanent source of income.
  • Funding Small Projects: Assisting in starting small projects by providing equipment like sewing machines (مكائن و اجهزة للكسب الصغير).

3. Social, Psychological, and Health Support

  • Psychological Counseling: Providing support to strengthen them psychologically after the loss/separation of a spouse.
  • Strengthening Social Ties: Making them feel like a valued part of the community.
  • Healthcare: Providing them with medical treatments and services (الرعاية الصحية).

Project Narrative Summary

1. The Islamic Community in Sri Lanka (Page 3)

Sri Lanka's Muslim population, particularly in certain rural and vulnerable areas, faces significant challenges related to socio-economic stability. The lack of access to quality education, limited health infrastructure, and a shortage of sustainable income sources are key issues that require targeted intervention. Al Hima focuses its efforts on empowering these vulnerable groups to achieve stability and prosperity.

2. Introduction to the Sponsorship Program (Page 4)

Al Hima's sponsorship initiative is comprehensive, focusing on long-term empowerment rather than just temporary relief. The primary target is the **Widows and Divorcees Sponsorship Program (Kafalatul Aramal)**, which provides financial aid coupled with vocational training.

3. The Critical Need for Sponsorship (Page 7)

Financial support is crucial for the continuation and expansion of this project. Your sponsorship of **$65 USD per month** directly covers food, clothing, and medical expenses, and helps fund vocational equipment (like sewing machines) necessary for beneficiaries to generate their own income.
