A Brief About Our Organizatio

Al Hima Islamic Services

Al Hima Islamic Services is a charitable and developmental organization dedicated to serving the Muslim community. Established by the grace of God and the efforts of dedicated volunteers in 2002 AD, we are registered under the Companies Act, Registration No: 3125/GA.

Our work is deeply rooted in Islamic values, ensuring comprehensive quality in all our endeavors—from education to health and social services. We aim to be a leader in community improvement and sustainable development.

Our Mission

Serving with Islamic Values

Our mission defines our present purpose: to be a contributing charitable society that delivers essential services rooted in the immutable principles of Islam.

AHIS is dedicated to actively engaging and supporting the Muslim community by providing comprehensive services across several key sectors:

Orphan Sponsorship Program

  • A primary focus is on sponsoring orphans (yatīm). This program covers their educational expenses, including tuition, books, and living costs, ensuring they receive the necessary tools to break the cycle of poverty and lead self-sufficient lives.

Widow Empowerment and Support

We prioritize providing direct financial and non-financial assistance to widows. This includes:
  • Monthly Stipends: Offering regular financial aid to help cover essential living expenses.
  • Skill Development: Implementing vocational training and small business startup support to help widows achieve economic independence and dignity.

Family Support Programs:

Implementing general welfare schemes such as food distribution, housing assistance, and seasonal aid (like during Ramadan and Eid ul-Adha) to assist vulnerable families and prevent destitution.

Our Vision

Leadership in Comprehensive Quality

Our vision is our aspiration for the future: to become the leading charitable and developmental organization in our operational scope, recognized for its efficiency, effectiveness, and comprehensive quality.

  • Achieve Leadership: Become the benchmark for excellence in providing charitable and developmental aid.
  • Ensure Comprehensive Quality:Integrate high-quality standards (Juda Shamilah) into every aspect of our work—from planning and execution to evaluation.
  • Deliver Integrated Services: Provide seamless services in the educational, developmental, social, and health sectors.
  • Improve Community Life: Ultimately, our goal is to significantly enhance the living standards, knowledge base, and overall well-being of the Muslim community we serve.
Our vision drives us toward long-term, sustainable impact, moving beyond immediate relief to establish lasting improvements within the community.

Our History

A Foundation Built on Faith (2002 AD)

Al-Hima Islamic Services (AIS) was established in 2002 AD through the grace of God and the collective efforts of dedicated and passionate volunteers. Recognizing the crucial need for integrated community support, these founders laid the groundwork for an organization deeply committed to uplifting the Muslim community.

We are officially registered under the Companies Act (Law No. 7 of 2007) of Sri Lanka, bearing the registration number 3125/GA. This legal foundation ensures that AHIS operates with transparency, accountability, and compliance under the oversight of the Registrar of Institutions, solidifying our commitment to structured and sustainable charitable work.
